# Code of conduct

Project spaces should be constructive, technically focused, and safe for contributors.

Be respectful. Critique work with specific evidence, assume good faith, and leave room for people
to learn. Harassment, threats, discrimination, doxxing, sexualized conduct, and sustained personal
attacks are not acceptable.

Maintainers may edit or remove disruptive content and may temporarily or permanently restrict
participation when needed to protect the project and its contributors. Report conduct concerns
privately through the repository's available contact or moderation tools.
